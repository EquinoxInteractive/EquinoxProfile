About Our Studio

Our Story

Equinox Interactive began its journey in April 2025 with “Box Siege,” a small project that sparked big ideas. With three core members at its heart, the studio thrives on a shared vision: creating fresh, engaging games that break conventions while keeping players smiling.

What started as a simple school project soon grew into something more Equinox Interactive emerged with a clear ambition: to build games that are not only polished and purposeful, but also fresh and original.

Our Vision

To be a creative hub for game developers to bring bold, imaginative ideas to life

Our Mission

To challenge the monotony of mainstream gaming by delivering original and meaningful experiences.

Box Siege

PvP Co-Op Puzzle

Box Siege is a vibrant 2D action platformer that began as local co-op PvP and now delivers a solo story mode with absurd characters, glitchy worlds, and two mind bending endings. Play as Box Bandel, a mischievous living cube, guided by an odd AI named Patch. Explore twisted dimensions, solve logic puzzles, and face a choice that defines everything: freedom or stability.
